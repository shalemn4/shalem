About US

Madadgaar Foundation is a compassionate NGO dedicated to fostering positive change in society. Our mission is driven by a commitment to women's empowerment, ensuring equal opportunities and rights. We passionately support child education, enabling young minds to thrive and contribute to a brighter future. Our efforts extend to animal welfare, advocating for the well-being of our furry friends. We are steadfast in our pursuit of uplifting livelihoods, alleviating poverty, and promoting sustainable economic growth. Additionally, we prioritize healthcare, working tirelessly to provide access to medical services for those in need.
